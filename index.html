Understanding Your BMI

BMI is a screening tool that uses your weight and height to gauge if you may be at risk for health problems related to being overweight or underweight.

Under 18.5 — Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 — Normal Weight ✓
25 – 29.9 — Overweight
30 – 34.9 — Obese Class I
35+ — Obese Class II+

Udaipur's Premium Fitness Destination

The Alpha Fitness V2 (দি আলফা ফিটনেস ভি2) was founded by Shubhanjan Das with a vision to bring championship-level training to the heart of Udaipur, Tripura. Situated conveniently inside the Union Bank of India building on New Town Road, we've built a community of dedicated fitness enthusiasts who train with purpose.

We pride ourselves on offering world-class equipment, expert coaching, and an inclusive environment where both beginners and seasoned athletes feel at home. Our philosophy is simple: train smart, train hard, and see results.
